📌 Level 00 — Orientation · L0.03 — The Deal Lifecycle

Every major transaction follows a recognisable arc — from the moment a client decides to transact, to the day the deal closes. Understanding this arc is the context in which every piece of IB technical work sits. Know where you are in the process at all times.

Total Timeline

A mid-market M&A process typically takes 5–7 months from mandate to close. A large-cap deal with regulatory complexity can take 12–18 months. An IPO preparation takes 6–12 months from kick-off to admission. Analysts on a single deal can spend six months of their life on one transaction — understanding the full arc helps you see how individual tasks connect to the whole.

In a Real Bank

An analyst is typically on 3–6 live transactions simultaneously — at different stages. You might be building the CIM for one deal, managing the data room for another, and preparing management for roadshow on a third. The ability to context-switch rapidly while maintaining quality on every deliverable is one of the most important skills you develop in the first year.
